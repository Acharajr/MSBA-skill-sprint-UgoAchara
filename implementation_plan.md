
## What does this repo do in plain language?

This repo contains a collection of projects designed to help build a strong data portfolio, whether you are interested in SQL or Python. It offers a wide range of practical examples. For this review, I focused on a project centered on scraping data from a website. I had previously completed Track 1 in earlier questions and wanted to see how these concepts work in real time. Amazon was a natural choice due to the scale and structure of its data.
